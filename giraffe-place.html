GIRAFFE ENCLOSURE

Our state-of-the-art giraffe enclosure allows you to get up close with these gentle giants. The habitat features acacia trees, a watering hole, and a special feeding platform where visitors can interact with the giraffes.

Daily schedule:

  • 9h30am : Morning feeding session
  • 11h00am : Keeper talk about giraffe conservation
  • 13h30pm : Giraffe enrichment activities
  • 15h30pm : Afternoon feeding session

Visitor experiences:

  • Feeding platform (additional fee)
  • Photography opportunities
  • Educational displays about giraffe biology

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The giraffe enclosure is located in the heart of the African Savannah section (Area A1 on the map), near the main entrance.
