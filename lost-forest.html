LOST FOREST ADVENTURE

Explore our immersive forest experience featuring rope bridges, tree platforms, and hidden animal encounters. This interactive exhibit lets you discover forest ecosystems from around the world.

Daily Activities:

  • 10h00 : Guided Forest Walk
  • 12h00 : Canopy Discovery Tour
  • 14h30 : Nocturnal Animals Presentation
  • 16h00 : Forest Scavenger Hunt

Features:

  • 300m of elevated walkways
  • Interactive learning stations
  • Native forest animals
  • Shaded picnic areas

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is located in the northern section of the zoo (Area L3 on the map), between the Asian Forest and Primate Pavilion.
