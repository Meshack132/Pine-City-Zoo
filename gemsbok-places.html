GEMSBOK HABITAT

Experience the beauty of the Kalahari Desert right here in our zoo! Our carefully recreated gemsbok habitat showcases these magnificent antelope in their natural environment.

Daily activities:

  • 10h00am : Gemsbok feeding time
  • 12h30pm : Keeper talk about desert adaptations
  • 15h00pm : Guided habitat tour

Special features:

  • Authentic desert vegetation
  • Shaded viewing areas
  • Interactive information panels

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The gemsbok habitat is located in the African zone, adjacent to the lion enclosure (Area D4 on the map).
