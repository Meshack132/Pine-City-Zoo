MONKEY TRAIL ADVENTURE

Walk through our immersive primate habitat where monkeys roam freely above your head on a network of ropes and platforms. This unique walkthrough exhibit lets you observe various monkey species up close.

Trail Features:

  • 200m of shaded pathways
  • Four different monkey species
  • Interactive feeding stations
  • Educational displays
  • Canopy-level viewing platforms

Daily Events:

  • 10h30 : Morning Monkey Feeding
  • 13h00 : Primate Keeper Talk
  • 15h30 : Enrichment Activities

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail connects the African and Asian sections of the zoo (Area T5 on the map), near the Lost Forest.
